About the Developers

Emma Egstad Github LinkedIn

Emma is a web developer in Portland, OR. She loves to make pixel art of all kinds, but especially super adorable pixel cats. In addition to pixel art, Emma loves CSS, array methods, and weekly builds (just kidding).

Fora Heims Github LinkedIn

Fora is a web developer in the Portland, OR area. Pixel art and chiptune have set the scene for Fora's adventures through life, from a Gameboy and Pokemon Gold, to Minecraft and Valheim. This project aligns pixel perfectly with Fora's passion for technology, art and logic.

Ian Christiansen Github LinkedIn

As a software developer and lover of the arts, PIXL is a perfect blend of Ian's interests. When he's not programming, you'll likely find him drawing, hunting down new music, playing games (both 8-bit and not), and exploring the wilds of Reno, NV and beyond...

Violet Stewart Github LinkedIn

Violet is a web developer who may or may not be made of hundreds of pixels herself. She loves to make art in all forms and wanted to work on this project to use a creative approach to make making art more accessible to those who balk at the thought of picking up a paintbrush. She is currently located in Portland, Oregon.
